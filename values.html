Our Identity

Art should
adapt to people,
not the opposite.

SHAART is an AI-powered art rental platform connecting independent artists, businesses, and individuals. We believe in making art accessible, flexible, and alive — for everyone.

"SHAART is an art rental platform powered by a community of artists that uses AI-powered tools to enable everyone to access art with flexible rentals."

SHAART Mission Statement

What we stand for

01

Accessibility First

Art has always been locked behind high prices and exclusive ownership. We believe experiencing original art should be a right, not a privilege — available to renters, offices, hotels, and anyone who wants to live beautifully.

02

Flexibility Over Ownership

The traditional model forces a permanent decision. SHAART lets spaces evolve — rotate artworks monthly, refresh a room's mood, adapt to seasons or brand identity. Art should move with life, not freeze it.

03

AI-Powered Personalisation

Every space has a personality. Our AI reads your room — its colours, mood, and character — and curates artworks that genuinely fit. Not generic suggestions. A real match, unique to your space.

04

Supporting Independent Artists

We partner with emerging and independent artists who deserve consistent income and visibility. Every rental on SHAART directly supports a creator — giving them a sustainable way to share their work with the world.

05

Designed for Accessibility

We pursue B Corp values in everything we build — ethical partnerships, transparent pricing, and a platform designed to remove barriers rather than create them. Art rental should be simpler, not harder.

06

Art That Is Alive

Static decoration is a thing of the past. SHAART believes spaces should breathe and change — telling new stories each season, reflecting how people grow, and keeping the experience of art genuinely exciting.

How we work

From artist studio
to your space

1

Curated Artist Network

We partner with independent artists and certified curators across the UK and Europe — vetting every piece for quality, originality, and story.

2

AI Room Matching

Our AI reads your room's colours, mood, and style — then recommends artworks that genuinely fit your space, not just popular pieces.

3

Flexible Rental Plans

Choose monthly, quarterly, or custom durations. Rotate whenever you want. No long-term commitment, no ownership headaches, no surprises.

4

Delivery & Collection

We handle everything — professional packaging, insured delivery, and collection when you're ready to swap. You just enjoy the art.
